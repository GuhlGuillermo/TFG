Create a new preliminary review request by uploading your manuscript and providing the required information. This option starts a fresh submission that will be processed independently from previous versions.

Upload a new version

Submit an updated version of an existing manuscript. Use this option when responding to reviewer feedback or refining a previous submission. The system will track and compare all uploaded versions.

Access the history of your submissions, review past analyses, and download previous reports. This section allows you to revisit completed evaluations and monitor your submission progress.
